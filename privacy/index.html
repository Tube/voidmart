VOIDMART — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

VOIDMART collects no personal data. The game is a self-contained app with no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no backend server that receives your information.

No data collected · No data shared · No tracking

What we collect

Nothing. VOIDMART does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or device information. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party analytics.

Data stored on your device

The game may store gameplay data (such as settings or progress) locally on your device so the app works offline. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Third-party services

The app loads web fonts from Google Fonts to render its interface. When fonts are fetched, Google may receive standard network request information (such as your IP address) as described in the Google Privacy Policy. Fonts are cached for offline use after first load. VOIDMART itself sends no other network requests and shares no data with any third party.

Children's privacy

VOIDMART does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected, none can be associated with a child.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
